Install the PWA

Installing adds an app icon to your home screen and helps the map stay available offline after you load it once online.

Android (Chrome)
  1. Open this site in Chrome.
  2. Tap the menu (⋮) in the top-right.
  3. Tap Install app (or Add to Home screen).
  4. Confirm.
iPhone / iPad (Safari)
  1. Open this site in Safari (not inside another app’s browser).
  2. Tap the Share button (square with an up arrow).
  3. Scroll and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Name it and tap Add.

Tip: After installing, open the app once online until the header says offline-ready.
